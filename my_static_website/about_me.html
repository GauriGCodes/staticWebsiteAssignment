Gauri Gupta

Hello, I am Gauri from Rajasthan, India. I am currently pursuing my Graduate Degree in Computer Science at Pace University.

I completed my undergraduate in 2019 in India. After pursuing my undergrad degree I started working at Barclays Bank as a Graduate Analyst. I worked as a DevOps Engineer on multiple technologies like chef, jenkins and linux. Moreover, I was part of the team that handled the infrastructure of 70k servers. I worked outside of the business hours to support production systems and prevented many outages resulted in an improved customer experience.

In July 2020, I started worked as a full stack developer at Barclays Investment Bank. I worked on numerous technologies like Angular, Java, Springboot, Teamcity and Docker. I developed various projects like hiring status utility that allowed management to keep track of new joiners and interviews. Also, I created a log searching utility that allowed vendors and business Analyst to search server logs in a user friendly manner.

Apart from my work experience I love volunteering. I've been an active member of many NGOs in India. I've been part of LifeSkills by Barclays where we help underpriviledged kids with overall personality development. Also, I like exploring new places, meeting new people, watching tennis, dancing and spending time on reddit.
